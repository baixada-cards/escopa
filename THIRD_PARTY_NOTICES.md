# Third-party notices

Escopa does not vendor third-party audio, images, or fonts. Card and score
sounds are synthesized locally with the Web Audio API.

The browser application requests Cormorant Garamond, Kalam, and Inter from
Google Fonts. Font files are not stored in this repository; consult the
[Google Fonts repository](https://github.com/google/fonts) for the applicable
open-font license texts and author credits.

The exact development dependency graph and licenses are represented by
`pnpm-lock.yaml`. Baixada's own tokens and marks are consumed from the exact
public design-system commit recorded in `dependencies.lock.json`.
